Our Mission


IDC Digital is a bi-coastal post-production facility bridging cultural gaps with the art of storytelling and connecting global citizens through localization, distribution and accessibility.



Our culture


IDC is a company of collaboration, respect, encouragement, and empowerment. We celebrate our team members’ differences and value each individual’s unique point of view. This cooperative approach adds to our diverse, talented teams’ ideas and enhances each of our client’s finished products. We strive to connect the world and its societal gaps through our localization and distribution services, making first-rate content universally available in a multitude of languages and formats. IDC stands strong in recognizing that, in order to achieve such greatness in the world and marketplace, we must fulfill this social and moral responsibility as a corporation. To that end, we champion inclusion, diversity, and justice in the comprehensive strategies to address our thinking, advocacy, culture, talent, partners, vendors, and community.
